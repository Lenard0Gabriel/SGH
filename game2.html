ENDING

Our game is an adventure game that has 1 survivor and the enemies are the zombies.

Unlike our previous games, which are The Jungle Jumper and Zombie Shooter, there are more functions that will be added to this new game that we will be going to create.

It is a platform game with action, adventure, and horror elements.

The player or the survivor must stay alive and survive until he reaches the safe zone in order to move to another level. The movement is simple as well, like pressing the left and right buttons and space for a jump.

Applied Math Functionality

Instead of placing hearts, we will be using a health bar for our player and our enemy. We also would like to add a timer to our game, so it would be thrilling to play.

AI Functionality

The AI function in this game will include AI chase and AI path-finding.
